Egg Curry

Description

Egg can be boiled or cooked to eat it. some prefer to eat raw egg , i mean drinking the yoke breaking the shell(yawk

boling an egg is very easy for someone new too. Just put your eggs in vessle of water. put the vessle on your stove and wait 10 to 15 min. poof your eggs are boiled , remove the outer shell and your egg is ready to eat

egg curry is bit different. lets see how to prepare it

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • onions
  • tomatoes
  • spices
  • chillies

    • Steps

    1. put oil in the bowl and put it on the stove
    2. add spices,chillies,onions and let them fry for 3 mins under medium flame
    3. add some turmeric powder and ginger paste
    4. add tomatoes and let them boil for 5 to 10 min
    5. now break your eggs into the tomatoes and leave it for 5 to 10 mins
    6. now add required amount of chilli powder and salt and sit back for 2 min
    7. at last add spices and mix well
