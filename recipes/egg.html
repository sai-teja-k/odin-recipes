Egg

Description

Egg can be boiled or cooked to eat it. some prefer to eat raw egg , i mean drinking the yoke breaking the shell(yawk

boling an egg is very easy for someone new too. Just put your eggs in vessle of water. put the vessle on your stove and wait 10 to 15 min. poof your eggs are boiled , remove the outer shell and your egg is ready to eat

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • water

    • Steps

    1. Pour water into the vessle
    2. put your eggs in the vessle
    3. turn on the stove
    4. put the vessle on stove
    5. wait for 10 to 15 min
    6. remove eggs from water and remove the shell
